About Us

Established in 1990, Southern Precision Industries is a market leader in manufacturing moulding inserts, plug gauges, depth gauges, and precision fixtures. With 35+ years of expertise, we have successfully designed and installed thousands of gauges for leading automotive and engineering clients.

We pride ourselves on building long-term partnerships based on quality, trust, and dependability.

Infrastructure & Capabilities

Our facility is equipped with dedicated units for procurement, production, quality assurance, R&D, warehousing, packaging, and sales. This infrastructure enables us to deliver tailor-made solutions with uncompromising precision.

  • 1000+ fixtures & gauges installed
  • Trusted by top automotive & engineering industries
