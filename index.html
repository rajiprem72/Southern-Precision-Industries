Welcome to Southern Precision Industries

Established in 1990, Southern Precision Industries has been a pioneer in precision engineering and manufacturing solutions. With over 35 years of expertise, we specialize in producing Jigs, Fixtures, Tools, Gauges, and Machining Components for leading automotive and engineering industries.

Our success is built on a foundation of quality, reliability, and long-term client relationships. With modern infrastructure, advanced machinery, and a skilled workforce, we ensure world-class manufacturing that exceeds customer expectations.
